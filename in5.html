4. What were the different rounds in your selection process?
ANS. There were basically 3 rounds.
The first one was a common entrance test out of which some 10 people were shortlisted. The second round was Group discussion in 2 groups having 5 members each, out of which 4 people were selected for PI round.
Finally there was the PI round or the HR round where it is necessary that we must impress the HRs as much as we can.

7. Tell us something about the role of different projects and research papers.
ANS. Projects are important as you actually come to know about a topic in detail when you yourself have worked on it. 8. What is the importance of CGPA cut-off in placements?
ANS. CGPAs are important but not to a very great extent. During these 4 years one should try to have a CGPA above 7 and maintain it. Many companies have a cut off CGPA of 7 for appearing in their entrance tests. Some companies also have cutoff of 7.5. So one must always try to maintain his/her CGPA above 7.5. CGPA also give you an upper edge when there is a tie between two candidates of equal caliber.
9. Do the backlogs have a negative impact in placements?
ANS. One should clear all the backlogs before the placement sessions. Some companies are there whose criteria is no past backlogs. In order to avoid such rejections, one must avoid backlogs.
