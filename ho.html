    VISION


    As a technical society, EEESOC aims at preparing electrical
    students to become professionally competent engineers who
    exemplify the excellence of the institute through their
    practical knowledge and management skills.


    MISSION


      To provide opportunities to innovate and implement   one’s ideas:

    • To augment one’s creativity

    • To apply and utilize the knowledge to the best and fullest way possible

    • To bring about revolutionary changes in the electrical field

    To extend one’s potential by conducting:
    • Workshops

    • Mentorship programmes

    • Online tests

    • Group discussions

    • Technical events
